Name
ShapeZone
Description

Syntax

ShapeZone()
ShapeZone(name)
ShapeZone(name, renderer)
ShapeZone(x, y, width, height)
ShapeZone(x, y, width, height, renderer)
ShapeZone(name, x, y, width, height)
ShapeZone(name, x, y, width, height, renderer)

Parameters
name:
- String: The name of the zone, used for the draw() and touch(), etc methods

x:
- int: X-coordinate of the upper left corner of the zone

y:
- int: Y-coordinate of the upper left corner of the zone

width:
- int: Width of the zone

height:
- int: Height of the zone

renderer:
- String: The renderer used to draw this zone
